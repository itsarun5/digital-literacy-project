Stay Safe Online Checklist (For College Students in India)

Prevention Tips
1. 	Use strong, unique passwords for each account and update them regularly.
2. 	Enable two‑factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible to add an extra layer of security.
3. 	Be cautious with links and attachments in emails or messages—avoid clicking if unsure.
4. 	Keep software and apps updated to protect against known vulnerabilities.
5. 	Limit personal information shared online (like phone numbers, addresses, or ID details).
6. 	For UPI/Online Payments: Always verify the receiver’s name before confirming a transaction.
7. 	For UPI/Online Payments: Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
8. 	Use secure Wi‑Fi connections—avoid making financial transactions on public or open networks.

Reporting Channels
• 	National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
• 	Helpline: Dial 1930
